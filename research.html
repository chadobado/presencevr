Frequently Asked Questions

Where is it located?

User testing takes place at our locations in San Francisco, Los Angeles, San Diego, and Phoenix. You can choose between these cities to visit the most convenient location for you.

How long will it take?

It is a 60-minute, in-person interview.

Can I sign up for a future user test?

We are not currently accepting submissions. However, you can sign up for our email newsletter below to stay up-to-date with monthly updates and future opportunities.

Am I paid?

This preliminary round of user-testing is unpaid research donated by people just like you, a global community of volunteers dedicated to eliminating unnecessary suffering in the world. Candidates that are selected may have paid opportunities for longer form research in the future.

Am I eligible?

Participants will be exposed to sensitive intellectual property and patent pending confidential information so are required to sign a non-disclosure agreement, must not be bound by any conflicting agreements (such as with a current or former employer), and consent to having their interview recorded for internal use only.
