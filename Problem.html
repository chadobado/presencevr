Understanding the Invisible Epidemic

Mental illness impacts 1.2 billion people. 75% don't get help.

The barrier that gets in the way of life

Physical Health Risks

43% of all adults suffer adverse health effects from stress.

Premature Death

On average, it can reduce your life expectancy by 20 years.

Impaired Performance

Those with such symptoms are likely to make 60% more mistakes on the job.

Damage Relationships

81% of people say that the stress from their job affects relationships with friends and family.

Recent studies have revealed the
scope of the problem

Severe Stress

75% of adults reported experiencing moderate to high levels of stress in the past month.

Increasing Costs

Payers will spend 30% more on this than fighting cancer in 2020.

Serious Consequences

120,000 employee deaths due to workplace stress each year.

Impact on society

National Costs

Mental disorders cost national economies several billion dollars annually.

Productivity Loss

United States lost more than 4 million workdays and experienced 20 million “work cutback days” due to mental health.

Incarceration

Nearly 15% of men and 30% of women booked into jails have a serious mental health condition.
