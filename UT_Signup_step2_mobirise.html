What do we mean by "Mental Health"?

In this survey, we're going to ask you questions pertaining to your "mental health" so it's important to understand what we mean.

Managing your mental health can mean trying to overcome a personal problem or internal challenge that prevents you from living the life you want (such as stress, insomnia, depression).

If you don't feel like you are limited by internal challenges, "managing your mental health" can also describe the actions you take to reach the next level in your personal growth (such as discovering your purpose, reaching your peak performance, or transcending your limits).
